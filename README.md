`landest` is an `R` package to estimate survival and a treatment effect using a landmark estimation approach.    

More details on the method are available in (1) Parast, L., Tian, L., & Cai, T. (2014). Landmark Estimation of Survival and Treatment Effect in a Randomized Clinical Trial. Journal of the American Statistical Association, 109(505), 384-394, [doi:10.1080/01621459.2013.842488](https://doi.org/10.1080/01621459.2013.842488),
and (2) Parast, L. & Griffin B.A. (2017). Landmark Estimation of Survival and Treatment Effects in Observational Studies. Lifetime Data Analysis, 23:161-182, [doi:10.1007/s10985-016-9358-z](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10985-016-9358-z).
